Breinvoer — Brain Food
VPRO × Herc Amsterdam  —  2023

Commissioned by Herc, an Amsterdam-based creative agency, to create artist idents for VPRO's Breinvoer (Brain Food) campaign — positioning VPRO as a broadcaster that feeds curious minds. Drawing from earlier surreal aesthetic work, the idents embody VPRO's reputation for imaginative, challenging content. The brief: viewing the world through the legs of VPRO.

The campaign ran across broadcast, festival, and print activations throughout 2023, appearing at IDFA, Lowlands Festival, and Dutch Design Week.
