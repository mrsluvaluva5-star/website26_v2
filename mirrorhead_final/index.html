Music Head

A digital face mirrors your expressions in real time. Smile, frown, blink, open your mouth, tilt your head — each movement generates sound and colour.

Head turns play xylophone and piano. Tilting triggers pan pipes. Smiling produces a warm shimmer, frowning a low drone, jaw opening an angelic choir, and blinking a percussive flash.

Your camera is used only to track facial movement — nothing is recorded, stored, or transmitted. All processing happens locally in your browser. Once loaded, this piece works offline.

Camera access is required. You may stop at any time by closing the page.
